Online Examination Monitoring

Professional supervision for external online exams.

ExamGuard provides a controlled online examination environment focused on webcam monitoring, browser activity detection, warning enforcement, and violation logging. Professors may embed exams from platforms such as Google Forms, Kahoot, Wayground, or other assessment tools while students complete them under camera and activity monitoring.
